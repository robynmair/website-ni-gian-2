Target audience and their needs

○ The target audience of our channel is teenagers, students, gamers, and investors.  They all need to be aware of the situation in Sitio Bebe. Investors must also be interested in our plan to help Sitio Bebe for them to be more willing to invest in our concert.

Why we chose gaming as our final content type

○ We chose gaming as our final content category because it is a great way to express the objective of our plan for Sitio Bebe. Additionally, it demonstrates creativity, which may more likely draw the attention of people and help with the promotion of our fundraising concert.







Quarter 2




Our brand name is Ikigai. Ikigai is a Japanese word meaning your life's purpose or bliss.

To represent different components of a whole, we'll use numerous triangles in the colors black and white. It's related to the saying "two heads are better than one" because when the triangles are merged to form our logo, they're all together in a group, creating one simple and appealing logo.

We'll use the tagline "It doesn't take much to help and assist" because we've all experienced hardship. We understand what Sitio Bebe is going through right now and are willing to assist them because we have all needed help at some point in our lives. Even if our life is challenging sometimes, helping and supporting others doesn't require much because it is hard work we are willing to do.
